"Every dish I make carries a piece of my soul—because food isn’t just nourishment, it’s love served on a plate."
– Chef. Enzo Romano

"Hey there, I’m the chef behind this space, and I’m really glad you’re here. This whole journey started with something simple—a deep love for coffee and comfort food that brings people together. I’ve spent years in kitchens, learning, failing, starting over, and pouring everything I had into every dish and every cup. There were moments when I wasn’t sure it would work—late nights, tight budgets, and a lot of self-doubt—but I always came back to the idea that food and coffee can do more than just fill you up. They can create moments, spark conversations, and make people feel something real.

That’s what this website is about. It’s not just a place to order—it’s a place built on heart, stories, and a little bit of hustle. Every recipe, every roast, every detail has a piece of me in it. I hope when you taste our food or sip our coffee, you feel that. Because more than anything, I want you to feel at home here. Thanks for being part of the journey."
